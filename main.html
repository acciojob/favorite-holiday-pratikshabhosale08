Turkey


Turkey is an upper-middle-income and emerging country; its economy is the world's 16th-largest by nominal and 11th-largest by PPP-adjusted GDP. As the 15th-largest electricity producer in the world, Turkey aims to become a hub for regional energy transportation.

  1. Hagia Sophia is a magnificent architectural marvel in Istanbul that transitioned from a cathedral to a mosque.
  2. Experience a breathtaking hot air balloon ride over the unique fairy chimneys of Cappadocia.
  3. Walk through the thermal mineral waters and stunning white travertine terraces of Pamukkale.
  4. Explore the remarkably preserved ancient Greek and Roman ruins of the Ephesus Ancient City.
  5. Wander through the opulent courtyards and historic treasures of the Topkapi Palace Museum.
  • Kebabs Grilled meats seasoned with local spices, cooked on skewers or vertical rotisseries.
  • Baklava Layers of crisp filo pastry filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with syrup.
  • Lahmacun Thin, crispy flatbread topped with a minced meat mixture, herbs, and lemon juice.
